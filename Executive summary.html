Situation
The Google Merchandise Store conversion rate sits at 1.4%, below the e-commerce benchmark of 2 to 3%. Analysis of 4.3 million events reveals that 98% of potential customers are lost before reaching checkout. The primary obstacle is not payment friction but product discovery. Only 1.9% of users have ever made a purchase, and retention drops below 1% by week four.
